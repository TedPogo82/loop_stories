You choose left!

You turn left and head down the corridor, which curves around a slow corner. As you walk further, you hear voices from an emerging room at the end of the hall. Peering in, you see a brightly lit room, filled with treasures, statues, ornate lanterns, and most importantly: the gang of thieves holding your wife hostage.

In the middle of the room sitting on an altar is a golden statuette. You see it is a small version of The Kritios Boy, the hidden artifact of Dionysus! The thieves are standing around their leader, who is nearing the altar. Holding your wife is one of the thieves. They all have their attention to the treasure, but your wife spots you, and here eyes widen. The look on her face clearly shows desperation.

However, nobody has spotted you yet. You tiptoe into the room and slide into the shadow of a large statue behind a pile of gold. Next to you are 2 levers.

You pull the left lever.

You pull the right lever.

You do nothing and wait for their next move.
