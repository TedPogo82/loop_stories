You stay hidden up in the balcony, watching their next move.

You decide that ambushing them is out of the question, since you only have a knife, and they have guns and outnumber you. So you wait, hoping for an opportunity to grab your wife and escape. You wait behind the balcony ledge and watch the man with the fedora hat approach the altar. He steps towards it, about to grab the treasure, when the floor gives out, dropping everyone down a chute, including the artifact. Their screams echo as they freefall down. As the only one left in the room, you look down to see they have landed in a large cavern of water. You watch them swim to the edge of the massive pool and crawl onto a platform. However, your wife is struggling to swim since her arms are tied.

You think of dropping down, when you see a massive shadow swimming in the water. It circles your wife, curious. Without any more hesitation, you jump in.
